ABOUT US

Who is

 AWSUG.PH?

AWS User Group Philippines (AWSUG.PH) is a Philippine based community united by its members’ passion for Amazon Web Services and its related tools and technologies including cloud computing, design implementation and servicing, high scalability, performance computing, production use cases, blockchain, Artificial Intelligence and so much more. This is a space where software developers, architects and engineers, IT team leaders and practitioners, cloud specialist and consultants, entrepreneurs as well as students learn from and network with peers to gain opportunity and knowledge with AWS latest tools and technologies. The AWSUG.PH community is the most active and one of the largest in the Asia Pacific. With the Philippines being awarded AWSUG Group in Asia Pacific 2022.
